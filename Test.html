About the Event

The Coding & Debugging Activity strengthens analytical thinking, real-world programming skills, and teamwork. Students solve algorithmic challenges, fix broken code, and compete to showcase their skills.

Participants gain exposure to coding standards, debugging techniques, collaboration, and fast problem-solving. Prizes, certificates, and recognition motivate students to excel further.

Event Categories

  • Coding Challenge: Time-based algorithmic problems in C/C++/Java/Python.
  • Debugging Challenge: Fix bugs, optimize logic, clean broken code.
  • Team Collaboration: Work in groups of up to 3 students.
  • Workshops: Best practices and hands-on guidance from faculty mentors.
